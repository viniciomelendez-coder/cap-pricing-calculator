📦 Blank Cap Costs
▼
ConfirmedEditable

Source: CapBargain.com (OTTO CAP)
For 1–5 pcs (no wholesale min at CapBargain), entry-level OTTO CAP structured caps start at $5.61–$5.75/pc. That's your real menudeo baseline.

For 6–20 pcs, small-volume pricing kicks in: $5–$7.50/pc. At 20+ pcs, wholesale pricing: $4–$6.50/pc. Premium caps (Richardson 112, Flexfit) run $8–$14/pc at wholesale.

The calculator uses slightly conservative numbers to account for color variability and quality consistency across orders.

🎨 DTF Transfer Costs
▼
ConfirmedEditable

Source: Ninja Transfers
Gang sheets price at $0.02/sq inch. A 4"×4" cap-front design = 16 sq in = $0.32 at max bulk. But with gang sheet waste, small runs, and ordering minimums, real cost is $1.80–$2.75/pc.

Small orders (1–5 caps) cost more per transfer because you can't fill a full gang sheet. This is already factored into the range-based pricing.

📊 Are My Prices Competitive?
▼
Market Research

What the market charges for custom DTF caps:
Single cap: $22–$35 retail
Small run (6–20): $15–$25/pc
Mid bulk (21–50): $14–$22/pc
Wholesale (100+): $9–$18/pc

Your Medium margin lands 10–20% below average market — competitive and profitable. Not the cheapest (which signals low quality) and not the most expensive (which loses price-sensitive clients).

Use Soft to win new clients or close large volume. Use Aggressive for rush orders, complex designs, or repeat clients who already trust you.

👐 Labor & Extras
▼
EstimatedEditable

Labor: ~3–6 min/cap at $15–18/hr = $0.75–$1.50/pc. Gets faster with volume.

Extras: Electricity ($0.10–0.20), poly bag + tissue ($0.20–0.35), handling time, tape. Total: $0.50–$1.00/pc.

Waste/Shrink: Failed presses, misaligned transfers, defective blanks (~2–5% rate). Modeled as $0.18–$0.70/pc by volume.
